Playoffs - Updated: 8 Aug 2013

2013 Playoffs

UPDATE

The final schedule for the 2013 season has been published. The schedule has been tweaked to accomodate the teams requesting late games only. Also, some dates later in the schedule were shuffled slightly since we are starting a week later than we had originally hoped for.

This season, we will again break the league up into 3 groups based upon the final standings from our regular season round-robin. Group A will consist of the top 4 teams, Group B of the next 5 teams and Group C of the bottom 4 teams. Each group will play one game against the other team in their group. Group winners will be the team with the best record at the end of this round robin. No game results from the regular season will carry forward into the playoff round robin.

Teams in Group A and C will play a single game each week. Group B will see 4 of 5 teams play on a given group day with a single game in a week for 3 weeks and one week with 2 group days.

Game results are to be submitted online, but use the report playoff game link. Standings will be published after all games for the week have been completed. Rainouts or game rescheduling can still occur. Some teams have indicated they have upcoming company events that affect a number of their players. If both teams involved in the game agree to reschedule, then submit it as a rainout. If you do it soon enough, the game can be rescheduled within the same week it was originally scheduled to avoid, when possible, teams having 2 games in a week.

To view the schedule, go to the Schedule page and select one of the links in the Playoff Tournament section.
