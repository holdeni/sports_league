Season Start DELAYED (Again)! Updated: 5 May @ 4pm

The City of Ottawa is now delaying the use of all sports fields until Tuesday (May 10th). This has caused us to reschedule the 2 games originally slated for Monday, May 9th.

The following games have now been moved and the team contacts advised. Online schedules are up-to-date. 

   Willy's Pizza vs Slime - now on Tue, May 10th at 6:30 North 

   RBC Lions vs Century 21 (Tim McLean) - now on Mon, May 16th at 6:30 North

NOTE: Many people have pointed out that the PSA put out by the city mentions sports fields are not to be used until May 15th. Dennis has talked with our city representative and has confirmed that "sports fields" means non-ball diamonds. At this time, the city is asking we don't use the ball diamonds until Tuesday, May 10th. If all goes well, we shall be able to start our games Tuesday evening.
