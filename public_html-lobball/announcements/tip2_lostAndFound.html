Site Tip: Reporting Items Lost Or Found At Diamonds - Updated: 12 June 2012

If you find an item left behind at the diamonds or have left something behind yourself, we ask that you use either the Team Contacts or the general mailing list to send out a notice. If you know when the item was lost or found, please consult the league schedule and send your notice to the specific teams that could have been at the diamond. If you are unsure when the item might have been lost, sending an email to the general league mailing list is allowed but the message will be delayed as it must be reviewed by the list moderator before it actually gets out to the list membership.

To learn more about the Team Contacts or general Mailing list, review the tip on Maintaining Your Contact Details.

Effective 12 June 2012 anyone sending emails to the league executive about lost and found items will be referred to this tip. Everyone has the ability to contact the appropriate teams.
