New this year is a change in the process for proposing league rule or bylaw changes. To facilitate discussion on rule proposals, all rule changes must be submitted to the exec (by email or dropped off) at least a week before the meeting. In your submission, please clearly indicate what current situation or rule you would like to modify and your wording for any modification. This process will give the exec a chance to organize the changes, clarify any issues we may see with the wording beforehand and handout the changes so that everyone can review and make a decision more easily. No NEW rule changes will be accepted from the floor this year, you must submit your proposal as noted above. Tweaks or amendments to proposals that are brought to a vote in the meeting will be acceptable from the floor.
